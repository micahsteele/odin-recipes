Meat Sandwich

Description

A quick and hearty meal.

Don't let how fast this meal comes together decieve you into thinking any less of it. This sandwich packs plenty of flavor and ends up punching way above its weight class.

Ingredients
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/2 an onion sliced thinly
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 3 slices of mortadella
  • 3 slices of capocollo
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
Steps
  1. Put the deli meats onto a warmed up skillet until the meat begins to warm
  2. Mix the onion, spinach and vinegar in a bowl
  3. Toast bread
  4. Spread mustard on the bread
  5. Put the salad mix on the bread and then the meat ontop of that
  6. Enjoy!
